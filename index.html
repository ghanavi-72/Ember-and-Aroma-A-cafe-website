Our Story

At Ember & Aroma, every cup tells a story and every corner whispers warmth. Nestled in the heart of the city, we’re a sanctuary of slow mornings and quiet conversations.

Our founders imagined a place where rich coffee aromas blend with the gentle crackle of a fireplace. We source small-batch roasts and bake pastries inspired by heirloom recipes. Come for the flavor, stay for the embrace.

— a second home for dreamers.
